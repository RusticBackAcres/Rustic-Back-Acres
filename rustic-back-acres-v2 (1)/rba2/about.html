About Rustic Back Acres

Who We Are

The Farm

Rustic Back Acres Farm began in 2020 with a simple dream and four goats. What started as pets to fulfill a long-held wish quickly grew into something more. As our love for these animals deepened, so did our herd.

We expanded beyond goats, welcoming miniature cattle — Jerseys, Highlands, Herefords, and Aberdeens — along with Kune Kune pigs and poultry. Our growing farm family eventually outgrew our space, leading us to Brighton, Tennessee, where we built our home and established Rustic Back Acres Farm as it stands today.

Our foundation is built on faith, family, and a commitment to quality breeding. We focus on ADGA and AGS-registered Nigerian Dwarf goats, carefully selecting for excellent conformation, strong milk production, and sound genetics.

At Rustic Back Acres, we're dedicated to raising healthy, well-bred animals that meet the highest standards. We're passionate about our animals and committed to excellence in everything we do.
