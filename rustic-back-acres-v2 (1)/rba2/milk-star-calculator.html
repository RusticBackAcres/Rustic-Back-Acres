ABOUT ONE-DAY TESTS
One-Day Milking Competitions are ADGA-sanctioned events (often held at shows). The doe is milked 3 times over 24 hours; milk is weighed and sampled by a DHI supervisor. Stars earned this way are ST *M — they pass to bucklings if the sire is *B or +B, but production values are not used in genetic evaluations and the doe cannot earn an AR designation from a one-day test alone. Source: 2025 ADGA Guidebook, Article IX Section F.

DOE MILK STARS — *M

AR *M (Advanced Registry) — Earned on DHIR continuous test. A doe qualifies on milk, BF, or protein alone. The star only passes to bucklings if she earned milk volume AND butterfat or protein. Requires DCR ≥ 75 (OS-40) and an appropriate Verification Test.

ST *M (Star Program) — Earned via one-day milking competition or OS-40 ST owner sampler. Not a lesser designation than AR. ST stars convey to bucklings with the right sire.

How a doe earns her *M:
(1) By meeting minimum DHIR production requirements (Section E) including DCR ≥ 75, appropriate VT, and documentation of the qualifying completed lactation, OR
(2) By earning 18+ points (standard) or 7+ points (miniature) at a recognized ADGA Milking Competition, OR
(3) On the basis of an AGS Advanced Registry or Canadian R.O.P. certificate where production meets ADGA minimums in 305 days or less.

BUCK DESIGNATIONS — FROM 2025 ADGA GUIDEBOOK

*B (Star Buck) — automatic, no fee — A buck earns his *B if EITHER of these is true:
(1) His dam is an AR doe that qualified on both milk AND butterfat, or a ST doe, AND his sire is an AR Sire, a Star Buck, or a +B (ST).
(2) His dam is an AR doe that qualified on both milk AND butterfat, or a ST doe, AND his sire's dam is an AR doe that qualified on both milk and butterfat, or a ST doe meeting those same requirements.

+B (ST) — Star Sire, earned through progeny — A buck earns his +B (ST) designation if he has any ONE of the following:
(1) Three *M daughters from three different dams — at least two of the dams must be registered or recorded with ADGA (per 2025 Guidebook p.95), OR
(2) Two +B sons (ST), OR
(3) One AR son and one +B son (ST), OR
(4) One AR son and two AR and/or *M daughters, OR
(5) One +B son (ST) and two AR and/or *M daughters.

++B — Double Star Sire — Earned by qualifying a second time through any of the +B (ST) methods above using different progeny than what earned the first +B. Must be earned on the basis of both sons AND daughters.

+B (AR) — Advanced Registry Sire — Earned if he has: (1) three AR daughters from three different dams (at least two ADGA registered/recorded), OR (2) two AR sons that are AR Sires.

++B (AR) — Double Advanced Registry Sire — Must have BOTH: (1) three AR daughters from three different dams (at least two ADGA registered) AND (2) two AR sons that are AR Sires.

Source: 2025 ADGA Guidebook, Article IX Section G, pages 91–94 · For reference only · Always verify with ADGA
